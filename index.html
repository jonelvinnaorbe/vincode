About

I'm a part-time freelance web developer who specializes on theme and plugin development. For over 4 years, I've been working from a wide range of industries and is surely loving every minute of it. I'm a person with strong commitment to integrity and it's always been my passion to build semantic and highly reliable websites. I graduated from Polytechnic University of Philippines as a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. I have a wide range of technical skills both on frontend (HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Javascript/jQuery, Bootstrap) and backend (PHP, Ajax, Web APIs). Working with teams, I am also well-acquainted with various project management systems such as Basecamp, Asana, Podio, Active Collab and Trello. Let's Talk.
